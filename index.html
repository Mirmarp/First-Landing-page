    My name is Mirkka Pulkkinen. My background is from education field and now I am changing the career to the UX/UI designer.

    About me

    Why did I do Ironhack?

    I have always been interested in how people behave, learn and notice in different ways. I was studying education, student counselling and psychology but I wasn't sure where exactly I would like to work in the future. I was working for some years in school world but after some time I felt the work was missing something. In the end, I was invited to test a new program by a UX designer and I realised the work is exactly what I was looking for. Didn't take a long and I was already looking for a bootcamp to get into the designer world. Ironahck had a good reviews and I decided to apply. Not regretting that decicion for sure!

    What would I like to do in the future?

    My strongest skills are on UX and I would like to start my new career as a junior UX designer. My passion is also to learn UI so I wish the future job I would have a possibility to improve my skill on that area as well.
